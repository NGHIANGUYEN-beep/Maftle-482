How to Play Maftle

Objective

Your goal is to guess the secret item by crafting items using available materials. Each crafted item will give you clues about the secret item.

Game Modes

    Daily Mode: A new secret item is available each day. This item will always reset at 12:00am EST
    Infinite Mode: Play as many rounds as you like with randomly selected secret items to practive your skills.

Crafting Items

Select materials from your inventory to craft an item. Once crafted, the game will provide feedback on how similar your crafted item is to the target item.

Feedback System

After crafting an item, you'll receive feedback indicating how many attributes your crafted item shares with the secret item. Use this information to refine your guesses!

Winning the Game

You win by correctly guessing the secret item. The game will display a congratulatory message and start a new round in Infinite Mode or end your session in Daily Mode.
